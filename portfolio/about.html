Jack Gould

Hello, my name is Jack Gould and I am from Grand Rapids, Michigan. I have always had an interest in the art of writing and rhetoric since grade school. Whether I was tasked with writing an original short story, or analyzing the symbolic importance of a novel's literary elements, I have always fascinated with the way words affect people. Lately I have been interested in the place rhetoric has in the realms of non profits and community foundations. Currently I am a sophmore at Michigan State University who is double majoring in Professional Writing, with a focus in communities and cultures, as well as Comparative Cultures and Politics in the James Madison College.
