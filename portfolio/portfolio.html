MC 112 Research Paper

An analysis of how the success of black athletes in the Harlem Renaissance had greater implications outside of the sports realm. Focuses on the successes of Jack Johnson, and the Harlem Renaissance Five basketball team.

WRA 202 1st Project

An assignment where the goal was to analyze and describe the use of an organization's rhetorical resources. The organization I chose to analyze was the Grand Rapids Community Foundation.

MC 230: Rwanda Essay

A discussion of the cultural scripts that led to the Rwandan Genocide of 1994. Focuses on the how previous Hutu revolutions, as well as the hierarchal social structure established by European colonists, led to the genocide.

WRA 202 4th Project

A group assignment where we created a visual aid for MSU students who are a part of the CAMP Program. The aid helped inform students of the benefits of the Healthy Michigan and Ingham Health Plans, as well as show students where to go in order to treat certain ailments
